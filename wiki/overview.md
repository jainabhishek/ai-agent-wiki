# Overview

The top-level orientation page for this wiki and the home for the **current
thesis** — the evolving synthesis of everything ingested so far.

The LLM maintains this page. As sources accumulate, it grows into:

- **Scope** — what this wiki is about and what it deliberately excludes.
- **Current thesis** — the best current synthesis across all sources, revised as
  evidence accumulates. Cite the pages it rests on.
- **Key entities** — the most important [[entities]] (link to their pages).
- **Key concepts** — the central [[concepts]].
- **Open questions** — what's unresolved or contradictory; the lint pass feeds
  this.
